Industrial Safety Intelligence System

Explainable, Context-Aware AI for Real-Time Industrial Safety Decision Support

The Idea

Industrial accidents are rarely caused by a single equipment failure or a single sensor crossing a threshold. Instead, they occur when multiple weak warning signals—such as increasing gas concentration, abnormal vibration, rising temperature, worker proximity, active work permits, and equipment degradation—combine to create a hazardous situation.

Existing industrial safety systems monitor these signals independently, producing numerous isolated alarms. Operators are then left to manually determine which alarms are related, which are most critical, and what actions should be taken. This often results in alarm fatigue, delayed response, and increased accident risk.

Our solution is an AI-powered Industrial Safety Intelligence System that acts as an intelligent decision-support platform. It continuously fuses data from multiple industrial sources—including IoT sensors, CCTV cameras, thermal cameras, and PLC/SCADA systems—to develop a comprehensive understanding of the plant's current safety state.

Instead of simply generating alarms, the system simultaneously identifies what hazardous scenario is occurring and which factors are contributing to it. For example, rather than reporting only a "gas leak," the AI may determine that the incident is associated with rising gas concentration, abnormal pressure, valve degradation, and an expired maintenance permit. This provides operators with a much clearer understanding of the situation than a traditional alarm.

Once a hazardous scenario has been identified, the system evaluates its severity by assigning a risk level (Low, Medium, High, or Critical) together with a confidence score that indicates how certain the AI is about its assessment.

When multiple hazards occur at the same time, the system intelligently prioritizes them based on operational context rather than severity alone. Factors such as worker proximity, active work permits, asset criticality, operational state, and the rate at which the situation is escalating are considered. As a result, a medium-severity gas leak near personnel performing hot work may be prioritized above a high-severity equipment vibration alert in an isolated area.

A key feature of the system is its Explainable AI layer, which provides transparent reasoning behind every AI-assisted decision. Rather than displaying only a risk score, the system explains:

Which sensors contributed to the alert.

Which contributing factors were identified.

Why the risk level was assigned.

Why one alert received higher priority than another.

How confident the AI is in its assessment.


Based on this analysis, the platform provides Decision Support & Safety Recommendations, suggesting actions such as evacuating a hazardous zone, suspending hot work, inspecting specific equipment, reducing operating pressure, or notifying maintenance personnel. These recommendations assist operators in making informed decisions, while the final control action always remains with the human operator. The system does not autonomously shut down industrial equipment.

To improve situational awareness and post-incident analysis, every event is recorded in a Safety Timeline and Digital Safety Log, showing how the hazard evolved over time—from initial abnormal sensor readings to risk escalation and recommended actions. This supports regulatory compliance, auditing, and future safety improvements.

Unlike predictive maintenance systems that attempt to estimate when a machine will fail, this solution focuses on trend-based hazard detection. It identifies unsafe operational patterns by recognizing combinations of gradually changing conditions before they escalate into dangerous incidents, without making unsupported time-to-failure predictions.

Key Innovations

Multi-modal data fusion integrating industrial sensors, CCTV, thermal cameras, and PLC/SCADA data.

Scenario and contributing factor detection, identifying both the hazardous event and the multiple factors leading to it.

Context-aware risk prioritization, ensuring the most operationally critical hazards receive immediate attention.

Explainable AI, providing transparent reasoning behind every AI-assisted decision.

Decision support, delivering actionable recommendations while keeping humans in control.

Safety Timeline & Digital Safety Log, enabling traceability, compliance, and post-incident analysis.


Value Proposition

The proposed system transforms raw industrial data into prioritized, explainable, and actionable safety intelligence, enabling plant operators to detect hazardous situations earlier, understand why they are occurring, prioritize the most critical risks, and take faster, better-informed actions to improve industrial safety while reducing alarm fatigue and operational risk.
